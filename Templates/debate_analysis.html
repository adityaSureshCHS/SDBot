Debate Analysis Guidelines

Guidelines for Debate Recording

Clarity: Your argument should be clear and easy to understand.

Pace: Your argument pace should be moderate and engaging.

Tone: Your argument should be confident and persuasive.

How to start a Debate Recording

  • Press "Record new Debate" below to start a new debate recording.
  • Practice maintaining a consistent pace throughout the speech.
  • Your results, feedback, and recommendations will be displayed after you finish.
