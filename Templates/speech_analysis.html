Speech Recording Guideline

Tips for speech recording

Clarity: Your speech should be concise and easy to understand.

Pace: Your speaking pace should be moderate and engaging.

Tone: Your tone should be confident and persuasive, and fit correctly with the content of your speech.

Emotion: Your facial expressions should also reflect your speech's content and should display confidence and genuinity.

How to start recording:

  • Press "Record New Speech" below to start a recording
  • Practice maintaining a consistent pace throughout the speech.
  • Your results, feedback, and recommendations will be displayed after you finish.
